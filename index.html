Who We Are

A Brotherhood of the Inner Way

The Ancient Martinist Order Philippines (AMOPH) is a legitimate initiatic fraternity operating within the current of the historic Ordre Martiniste, dedicated to the spiritual and philosophical teachings of its founding masters.

We are not a correspondence course, a virtual fraternity, or a commercial enterprise. Our work is conducted in person, in the City of Manila, through genuine human transmission of the Martinist degrees by duly initiated and authorised members.

The path of the Unknown Philosopher is one of silence, study, and sincere aspiration toward divine reintegration.

Our Principles

The Foundation of Our Work

Authentic Transmission

We transmit the initiatic current through physical, human-to-human contact — the only form recognised by the historic Martinist tradition.

Freedom from Commerce

No fees, no subscriptions, no merchandise. The path of the Unknown Philosopher has never been bought or sold.

Philosophical Study

Members are encouraged in the serious study of Martinist texts, theurgy, and the Western esoteric tradition in all its breadth.

Fraternal Discretion

The Martinist ideal of the Unknown Philosopher implies a life of service lived quietly, away from ostentation or public spectacle.
